Understanding Alzheimer's Disease

This is a progressive brain disorder that gradually deteriorates memory, cognitive abilities, and the ability to perform everyday tasks. It is the most common cause of dementia, affecting millions of people worldwide. Alzheimer's primarily impacts older adults but can also affect people under 65 in what is known as early-onset Alzheimer's.

The Classic on the Dan event is dedicated to raising awareness and funds to support Alzheimer’s research.

Get Involved

By participating in this charity event, you’re making a difference. Every effort helps move us closer to a cure.

When you compete in the Classic on the Dan, you’re not just playing a game. Your participation contributes directly to vital Alzheimer’s research and support programs. Registration fees, sponsorships, and donations collected during the event go toward advancing treatment options, funding research, and providing resources to patients and caregivers affected by Alzheimer’s disease.

Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or new to the game, your involvement brings us one step closer to breakthroughs in treatment and care. With each swing, you help amplify awareness, fund crucial research, and support a network of families and caregivers who rely on these resources.
